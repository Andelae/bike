Mountain Bike

You don't mind getting dirty. A bumpy path is what you enjoy. Mountain bikes are capable of handling a variety of terrains depending on the type, and continue to be the go-to choice for biking enthusiasts who crave a little more adventure and action with their riding.

Mountain Bicycles are design for riding rough off-road trails. They have flat or upright handlebars, and a very low gear range for pedaling up steep trails. Most mountain bikes have some type of shock absorbers or suspension.
